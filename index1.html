About Our Zoo

Wildlife Zoo is dedicated to conservation, education, and fun. We house over 200 species in naturalistic habitats. Come visit us to learn about wildlife and support our efforts to protect endangered animals.

Featured Animals

Lion

The king of the jungle! Learn about their social behavior and habitats.

Elephant

Gentle giants with incredible memory. Discover their role in ecosystems.

Penguin

Adorable swimmers! Explore their Antarctic adventures.

Giraffe

Tallest mammals on Earth. Fun facts about their long necks!
